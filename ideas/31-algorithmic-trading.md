# Algorithmic Trading Bot Platform

## Overview
A platform for retail traders to create, backtest, and deploy algorithmic trading strategies without coding. Democratizes algo trading for non-programmers.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Algo trading growing at 11.1% CAGR
- **Trend**: 60-73% of stock trading is algorithmic
- **Barrier**: Requires programming knowledge (Python, APIs)

## Key Features
1. **No-Code Strategy Builder**: Drag-drop trading logic
2. **Backtesting**: Test strategy on historical data
3. **Paper Trading**: Practice with fake money
4. **Live Trading**: Connect to Alpaca, Interactive Brokers
5. **Strategy Marketplace**: Buy/sell winning strategies
6. **Risk Management**: Auto stop-loss, position sizing

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: Paper trading only
- **Starter**: $49/month - Live trading, 1 bot
- **Professional**: $149/month - 10 bots, advanced features
- **Strategy Sales**: 20% commission on marketplace

**Path to $100K MRR**: 700 users @ $143 average = $100K

## Risks & Mitigation
- **High Risk**: Trading losses, regulatory scrutiny
- **Mitigation**: Clear disclaimers, risk education, paper trading first
