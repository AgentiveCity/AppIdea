# Web3 Streaming Platform with Creator NFTs

## Overview
A decentralized streaming platform (Twitch/YouTube alternative) where creators earn through NFT subscriptions, token tips, and exclusive content gates. Built on blockchain.

## Key Features
1. **NFT Subscriptions**: Monthly NFT = membership (tradeable)
2. **Token Tipping**: Tip creators in crypto (instant, low fees)
3. **Token-Gated Streams**: Exclusive streams for token holders
4. **Creator Tokens**: Mint personal token, fans invest in your success
5. **Decentralized Storage**: Videos on IPFS/Arweave
6. **DAO Governance**: Community votes on platform features

## Revenue Model
- **Transaction Fee**: 5% on all payments (vs. 45-50% traditional)
- **NFT Minting**: $10 fee per NFT collection launch
- **Premium Tools**: $29/month for analytics, streaming tools

**Path to $100K MRR**: $2M/month creator earnings (5% = $100K)

## Challenges
- **User Experience**: Web3 wallets confuse mainstream users
- **Scalability**: Blockchain streaming is technically challenging
- **Regulation**: Crypto regulations may impact model
- **Network Effects**: Competing with YouTube/Twitch is extremely hard

## Competitive Advantages
- **Creator-Friendly**: 95% to creators vs. 50-55%
- **Ownership**: Creators own their fan relationships
- **Tradeable Subscriptions**: NFTs can be resold (unique)

## Realistic Assessment
This is highest risk/highest reward. Requires:
- $5M+ funding for infrastructure
