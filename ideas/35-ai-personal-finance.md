# AI Personal Finance & Budgeting App

## Overview
An AI-powered personal finance app that analyzes spending, provides personalized saving tips, and automatically optimizes budget. Think "Mint meets GPT financial advisor."

## Market Opportunity

- **Problem**: 64% of Americans live paycheck-to-paycheck
- **Opportunity**: AI makes financial advice accessible

## Key Features
1. **Auto-Categorization**: AI categorizes all transactions
2. **Spending Insights**: "You spent 40% more on dining out this month"
3. **AI Financial Coach**: Chat with AI for advice
4. **Bill Negotiation**: AI negotiates lower bills (cable, phone)
5. **Savings Automation**: Auto-transfer to savings based on patterns
6. **Debt Payoff Plans**: Avalanche/snowball method optimization

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: Basic budgeting, manual categorization
- **Premium**: $9.99/month - AI features, bill negotiation
- **Commission**: 30% of savings from bill negotiation

**Path to $100K MRR**: 10,000 Premium users @ $9.99 = $100K

## Competitive Advantages vs. Mint/YNAB
- **AI Coaching**: Interactive advice (Mint is passive)
- **Bill Negotiation**: Saves users money (competitors don't have)
- **Modern UI**: Mint is outdated, YNAB is complex
