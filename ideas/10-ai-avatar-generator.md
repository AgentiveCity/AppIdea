# AI Avatar & Digital Twin Generator

## Overview
A TikTok-viral AI avatar generator that creates personalized, animated digital twins from photos and videos. Users can generate realistic avatars that speak any language, perfect for content creators, businesses, and social media users.

## Market Opportunity

### Market Size
- AI avatar market: $7.2B by 2030 (42% CAGR)
- Creator economy: $250B globally
- TikTok has 1B+ active users seeking viral content

### Problem Statement
- Content creators need engaging videos but camera-shy
- Multilingual content creation is expensive
- Video production is time-consuming
- Personal brand scaling requires being on-camera 24/7
- Traditional avatar tools require technical skills

## Target Audience
- TikTok and Instagram content creators
- YouTube educators and course creators
- Business professionals needing video presentations
- International marketers (multilingual content)
- Virtual influencers and digital artists
- Corporate trainers

## Key Features

### Core Functionality
1. **Quick Avatar Generation**: Create AI avatar from 5-10 photos or 30-second video
2. **Text-to-Video**: Type script, avatar narrates in your voice
3. **Voice Cloning**: Match your natural voice and speaking style
4. **Multi-Language**: Speak any of 100+ languages with lip-sync
5. **Emotion Control**: Happy, sad, excited, professional expressions

### Premium Features
6. **Custom Backgrounds**: Green screen replacement, virtual studios
7. **Gesture Animation**: Hand movements, head tilts, natural gestures
8. **Batch Creation**: Generate 50 videos at once
9. **HD Quality**: 4K export for professional use
10. **Commercial License**: Use for client work and advertising

## Viral Mechanics

## Technical Stack
- **Frontend**: React Native (mobile-first), Web app
- **Backend**: Python (FastAPI), Node.js
- **AI/ML**:
  - Stable Diffusion for avatar generation
  - ElevenLabs/Resemble.ai for voice cloning
  - D-ID or Synthesia API for animation
  - Custom lip-sync models
- **Infrastructure**: AWS, CloudFlare for video delivery
- **Storage**: S3 for generated videos
