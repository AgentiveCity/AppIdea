# Local Home Services On-Demand Platform

## Overview
An on-demand platform for local home services (handyman, cleaning, plumbing, electrical). TaskRabbit meets Angie's List with instant booking and real-time tracking.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Home services market $600B+ in US alone
- **Problem**: Calling contractors, waiting for quotes is slow
- **Trend**: On-demand everything (Uber for home services)

## Key Features
1. **Instant Booking**: Book plumber in <5 minutes
2. **Real-Time Tracking**: Track provider like Uber
3. **Upfront Pricing**: See price before booking (no surprises)
4. **Background Checks**: All providers vetted
5. **Insurance**: Platform insurance covers damages
6. **Reviews**: Verified reviews from real customers

## Revenue Model
- **Commission**: 20-25% of job value
- **Service Fee**: $5-10 booking fee paid by customer
- **Lead Generation**: Providers pay for high-value leads

**Path to $100K MRR**: $500K/month in jobs (20% = $100K)

## Competitive Advantages
- **Instant Booking**: Thumbtack/Angie's List require quotes (slow)
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Competitors don't have this
- **Upfront Pricing**: Reduces anxiety

## Go-to-Market
1. **Recruit Providers**: Start with 50 in one city
2. **Density**: Own one city before expanding
3. **Local Ads**: Facebook, Google local targeting

## Timeline to $100K MRR: 18-24 months
