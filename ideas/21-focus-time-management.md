# Focus & Deep Work Time Management App

## Overview
A time management app that blocks distractions, schedules deep work sessions, and uses gamification to build focus habits. Combines Freedom, Forest, and Pomodoro techniques.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Productivity apps $12.26B growing to $29.56B
- **Problem**: Average worker switches tasks every 3 minutes, takes 23 minutes to refocus
- **Demand**: Focus/blocking apps have 10M+ downloads

## Key Features
1. **Smart Blocking**: AI blocks distracting apps/websites during focus time
2. **Deep Work Sessions**: Schedule 90-minute focus blocks
3. **Forest Gamification**: Grow virtual forest (tree dies if you get distracted)
4. **Focus Score**: Daily/weekly productivity scoring
5. **Ambient Sounds**: Curated focus music, nature sounds
6. **Analytics**: Time spent focused vs. distracted

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: 1 focus session/day, basic blocking
- **Premium**: $6.99/month or $49.99/year - Unlimited sessions, advanced features

**Path to $100K MRR**: 14,000 Premium users @ $6.99 = $98K

## Viral Mechanics
- **Focus Streaks**: "30 days of deep work" social shares
- **Leaderboards**: Compete with friends on focus time
- **Forest Sharing**: Share your beautiful forest growth

## Competitive Advantages
- **All-in-One**: Combines best of Freedom, Forest, Pomodoro
- **Cross-Platform**: Desktop + mobile (synced)
- **Gamified**: Makes boring task fun
