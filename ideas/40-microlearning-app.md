# Microlearning & Professional Skill Development App

## Overview
A microlearning app delivering bite-sized professional skills training in 5-10 minute lessons. Learn data science, marketing, leadership while commuting. Duolingo for professional skills.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: EdTech $163.49B growing to $348.41B
- **Trend**: Microlearning is top EdTech trend for 2025
- **Value**: 40-60% faster learning than traditional courses

## Key Features
1. **5-Minute Lessons**: Perfect for commute, coffee break
2. **Skills Path**: Structured learning paths (e.g., "Become Data Analyst")
3. **Daily Streaks**: Gamification drives daily habit
4. **Practice Exercises**: Hands-on practice, not just videos
5. **Certificates**: Earn certificates for completed paths
6. **Mobile-First**: Learn on phone, tablet, web

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: Access to limited skills paths
- **Premium**: $19.99/month - All skills, certificates
- **Business**: $15/user/month - Team analytics, custom content

**Path to $100K MRR**: 5,000 Premium users @ $19.99 = $100K

## Viral Mechanics
- **Streak Sharing**: "100-day learning streak!"
- **Certificate Sharing**: Post to LinkedIn
- **Progress Widgets**: Share knowledge graph

## Competitive Advantages
- **Micro Format**: Coursera/Udemy require hours, we need minutes
- **Habit-Forming**: Daily streaks (like Duolingo)
- **Professional Focus**: Not academic, real-world skills
