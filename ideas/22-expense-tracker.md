# AI Business Expense Tracker

## Overview
An AI-powered expense tracking app with OCR receipt scanning, automatic categorization, and real-time business insights. For freelancers, small businesses, and entrepreneurs.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Expense management software $7.4B (2024) → $14.7B (2030)
- **Target**: 60M+ freelancers/small businesses in US
- **Problem**: Manual expense tracking wastes 5 hours/month

## Key Features
1. **Receipt Scanning**: Photo of receipt → auto-categorized expense
2. **Mileage Tracking**: Auto-detect business trips (GPS)
3. **Tax Categories**: IRS-compliant categorization
4. **Real-Time Insights**: "You've spent $X on [category] this month"
5. **Export**: CSV/Excel for accountants, QuickBooks sync
6. **Multi-Currency**: For international businesses

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: 10 receipts/month
- **Pro**: $9.99/month - Unlimited receipts, mileage tracking
- **Business**: $19.99/month - Team features, multiple businesses

**Path to $100K MRR**: 10,000 Pro users @ $9.99 = $100K

## Competitive Advantages
- **AI-Powered**: Better OCR than Expensify
- **Affordable**: $10 vs. $25-50 for competitors
- **Simple**: No overwhelming features (Expensify is complex)
