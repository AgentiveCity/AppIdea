# Peer-to-Peer Lending Platform

## Overview
A P2P lending platform connecting borrowers with individual investors. Lower rates for borrowers, higher returns for investors than traditional banks.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: P2P lending $558B globally (2024)
- **Growth**: 29.7% CAGR through 2030
- **Value**: Borrowers save 2-5% vs. banks, investors earn 5-9% returns

## Key Features
1. **Auto-Investing**: Set criteria, auto-fund matching loans
2. **Risk Rating**: AI-powered credit scoring
3. **Diversification**: Invest $25 across 100+ loans
4. **Secondary Market**: Sell loan notes early
5. **Borrower Dashboard**: Track loan, make payments
6. **Collections**: Automated collections for late payments

## Revenue Model
- **Origination Fee**: 1-5% from borrowers
- **Servicing Fee**: 1% annual from investors

**Path to $100K MRR**: $20M loan originations/year @ 5% fee

## Regulatory Requirements
- State-by-state lending licenses (50 states)
- SEC registration for securities (loan notes)
- Cost: $2-5M initial setup
- Complexity: 24-36 months to launch

## Competitors
- LendingClub, Prosper (leaders)
- Opportunity: Niche focus (small business, medical, student loans)

## Timeline to $100K MRR: 30-36 months
