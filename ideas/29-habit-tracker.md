# Habit Tracking with Gamification

## Overview
A gamified habit tracking app that makes building good habits fun through RPG mechanics, social accountability, and AI coaching. Level up your character by completing real-life habits.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Habit tracking apps downloaded 100M+ times
- **Success**: Habitica (8M+ users), Streaks (top paid app)
- **Psychology**: 66 days to form habit, apps increase success by 3x

## Key Features
1. **RPG Gamification**: Complete habits → earn XP → level up character
2. **Habit Streaks**: Visual streak tracking, lose streak if you miss
3. **AI Coaching**: Personalized tips when struggling
4. **Social Accountability**: Share habits with friends, group challenges
5. **Rewards System**: Unlock avatars, themes, power-ups
6. **Habit Stacking**: Link habits together for bonus XP

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: 3 habits, basic avatar
- **Premium**: $4.99/month - Unlimited habits, premium avatars, no ads

**Path to $100K MRR**: 20,000 Premium users @ $4.99 = $100K

## Viral Mechanics
- **Character Sharing**: Show off your leveled-up character
- **Streak Milestones**: "365-day streak!"
- **Transformations**: Habit results (fitness, reading, etc.)

## Competitive Advantages
- **More Gamified**: Better than Habitica's outdated UI
- **AI Coaching**: Competitors lack personalized advice
- **Social**: Built-in accountability partners
