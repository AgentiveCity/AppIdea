# Vertical Freelance Marketplace

## Overview
A freelance marketplace focused on a specific niche (e.g., healthcare, legal, construction) rather than general freelancing. Better matching, higher quality than Upwork/Fiverr.

## Example Niche: Healthcare Freelancers
- Medical writers, healthcare consultants, telehealth doctors
- Hospitals/clinics need specialized talent

## Key Features
1. **Niche-Specific Profiles**: Tailored to industry (certifications, experience)
2. **Verified Credentials**: Check licenses, certifications
3. **Industry Tools**: Specialized tools for the niche
4. **Compliance Built-In**: HIPAA, industry regulations
5. **Network Effect**: All healthcare freelancers in one place

## Revenue Model
- **Commission**: 15% of freelancer earnings
- **Premium Listings**: Freelancers pay $29/month for featured placement
- **Hiring Company Subscription**: $99/month for unlimited job posts

**Path to $100K MRR**: $670K freelancer earnings (15% = $100K)

## Competitive Advantages
- **Specialization**: Better quality than Upwork generalists
- **Trust**: Verified credentials (Upwork doesn't verify)
- **Network**: Everyone in industry in one place
