# Hybrid Work Coordination Platform

## Overview
A platform that coordinates hybrid work schedules, office space booking, and team collaboration for companies with flexible office/remote policies.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Hybrid work tools $5B+ and growing
- **Trend**: 74% of companies implementing hybrid work (2025)
- **Problem**: Coordination chaos - who's in office when?

## Key Features
1. **Schedule Coordination**: See who's in office each day
2. **Desk Booking**: Reserve workspace, meeting rooms
3. **Team Sync**: "Find days your team is in"
4. **Calendar Integration**: Syncs with Google/Outlook
5. **Office Maps**: Interactive floor plans
6. **Analytics**: Space utilization, attendance patterns

## Revenue Model
- **Per Employee**: $5/employee/month
- **Enterprise**: $3/employee/month (500+ employees)

**Path to $100K MRR**: 20,000 employees @ $5 = $100K

## Competitive Advantages
- **Purpose-Built**: Not a retrofit of old tools
- **Employee Experience**: Focus on employee needs, not just admin
- **Affordable**: $5 vs. $10-15 for competitors

## Go-to-Market
1. **LinkedIn**: Target HR leaders
2. **Case Studies**: "How [company] solved hybrid chaos"
3. **Partnerships**: WeWork, Regus (coworking spaces)
