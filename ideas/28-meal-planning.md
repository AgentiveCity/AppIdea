# AI Meal Planning & Grocery App

## Overview
An AI-powered meal planning app that generates personalized meal plans, creates grocery lists, and offers budget-friendly recipes. Integrates with grocery delivery services.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Meal planning apps $400M+ growing rapidly
- **Problem**: Meal planning takes 2-3 hours/week, food waste costs $1,500/year
- **Trend**: Home cooking increased 50% since 2020

## Key Features
1. **AI Meal Plans**: Personalized for diet, budget, preferences, family size
2. **Auto Grocery Lists**: One-tap shopping list from meal plan
3. **Recipe Database**: 100K+ recipes with nutrition info
4. **Budget Optimization**: "Feed family of 4 for $50/week"
5. **Waste Reduction**: Use ingredients across multiple meals
6. **Delivery Integration**: Order groceries via Instacart/Amazon Fresh

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: 3 meal plans/month, basic recipes
- **Premium**: $7.99/month - Unlimited plans, premium recipes, grocery sync

**Path to $100K MRR**: 12,500 Premium users @ $7.99 = $100K

## Viral Mechanics
- **Before/After**: "Cut grocery bill by 40%"
- **Weekly Meal Boards**: Share beautiful meal plans
- **Success Stories**: Weight loss + money saved

## Competitive Advantages
- **AI-Powered**: True personalization (not templated)
- **Budget Focus**: Competitors don't optimize for cost
- **Grocery Integration**: One-tap ordering

## Timeline to $100K MRR: 10-14 months
