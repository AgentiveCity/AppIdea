# Team Knowledge Management System

## Overview
An AI-powered knowledge base that makes company knowledge searchable and accessible. Think "Notion meets ChatGPT" - ask questions, get answers from company docs.

- **Problem**: Information scattered across Slack, docs, wikis
- **Cost**: Employees spend 2.5 hours/day searching for information

## Key Features
1. **AI Search**: Natural language queries ("How do I...?")
2. **Auto-Indexing**: Scans Slack, Notion, Google Drive, Confluence
3. **Smart Suggestions**: "You might also need..."
4. **Version Control**: Track document changes
5. **Access Control**: Role-based permissions
6. **Usage Analytics**: What's being searched, by whom

## Revenue Model
- **Starter**: $99/month - 25 users
- **Business**: $299/month - 100 users
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing - 1,000+ users

**Path to $100K MRR**: 350 Business customers @ $285 average = $100K

## Competitive Advantages vs. Notion/Confluence
- **AI-Powered Search**: Understands context (Notion search is basic)
- **Auto-Organization**: AI categorizes content
- **Unified**: All knowledge in one place
- **Faster**: Find answers in seconds, not minutes
