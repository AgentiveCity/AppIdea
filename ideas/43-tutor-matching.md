# Student-Tutor Matching Platform with AI

## Overview
An AI-powered platform matching students with tutors based on learning style, subject needs, and personality fit. Uber for tutoring with AI optimization.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Private tutoring $140B globally
- **Growth**: Online tutoring growing 14.7% annually
- **Problem**: Finding right tutor is time-consuming, hit-or-miss

## Key Features
1. **AI Matching**: Match based on 20+ factors
2. **Video Sessions**: Built-in video calling
3. **Scheduling**: Auto-scheduling with calendar integration
4. **Progress Tracking**: Track student improvement
5. **Payment**: Escrow, auto-payment after session
6. **Reviews & Ratings**: Verified reviews from students

## Revenue Model
- **Commission**: 20% of tutor earnings
- **Premium Tutors**: 15% commission (incentive for top tutors)
- **Subscription**: Students pay $9.99/month for priority matching

**Path to $100K MRR**: $500K tutor earnings/month (20% = $100K)

## Competitive Advantages
- **AI Matching**: Better than manual browsing (Wyzant, Tutor.com)
- **Lower Commission**: 20% vs. 25-40% (competitors)
- **Built-in Tools**: Video, scheduling, payments in one place

## Go-to-Market
1. **Recruit Tutors**: Free to join, lower commission
2. **Parent Communities**: Facebook groups, local forums
3. **SEO**: "Math tutor near me" keywords
