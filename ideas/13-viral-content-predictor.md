# Viral Content Prediction Tool

## Overview
An AI tool that analyzes content before posting and predicts virality score (1-100). Suggests improvements to increase viral potential.

## Market Opportunity
- **Target**: Every content creator wants viral content
- **Problem**: Trial and error is inefficient
- **Validation**: "Going viral" = millions in value

## Key Features
1. **Viral Score**: Upload video/image, get score (1-100)
2. **Improvement Suggestions**: "Add text overlay," "Change thumbnail"
3. **Trend Analysis**: What's trending in your niche
4. **Optimal Length**: Best video length for platform
5. **Sound Recommendations**: Trending audio tracks
6. **Hook Analysis**: First 3 seconds rating

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: 3 analyses/month
- **Creator**: $14.99/month - 50 analyses
- **Pro**: $49.99/month - Unlimited, advanced insights

**Path to $100K MRR**: 6,700 creators @ $14.99 = $100K

## Viral Mechanics
- **Success Stories**: "This tool predicted my viral video"
- **Before/After**: Low score → make changes → goes viral
- **Leaderboard**: Highest viral scores shared

## Competitive Advantages
- **Prescriptive** (vs. descriptive analytics)
- **Pre-Post** (advice before publishing)
- **Simple** (one score, clear actions)
