# AI Language Learning with Conversation Partners

## Overview
A language learning app with AI conversation partners that adapt to your level. Practice real conversations with AI tutors 24/7. Duolingo meets ChatGPT.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Language learning apps $15B+ globally
- **Success**: Duolingo 500M+ users, $500M+ revenue
- **Gap**: Duolingo lacks conversational practice

## Key Features
1. **AI Conversation Partner**: Chat with AI in target language
2. **Real-Time Corrections**: AI corrects grammar, pronunciation
3. **Personalized Curriculum**: Adapts to your level, interests
4. **Speech Recognition**: Practice speaking, get feedback
5. **Cultural Context**: Learn idioms, cultural nuances
6. **40+ Languages**: Major languages + less common ones

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: 5 conversations/month
- **Premium**: $12.99/month - Unlimited conversations, all languages
- **Family**: $19.99/month - Up to 5 family members

**Path to $100K MRR**: 7,700 Premium users @ $12.99 = $100K

## Competitive Advantages vs. Duolingo
- **Conversational**: Real practice (Duolingo is repetitive exercises)
- **AI-Powered**: Personalized, adapts (Duolingo is one-size-fits-all)
- **Speaking Focus**: Practice speaking (Duolingo weak here)

## Viral Mechanics
- **Fluency Tests**: "I reached B2 level!"
- **Streak Badges**: Daily practice streaks
- **Conversation Sharing**: Fun AI conversation screenshots
