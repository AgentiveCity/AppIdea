# Multi-Platform Content Scheduler with AI

## Overview
An AI-powered social media scheduler that publishes to all platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, LinkedIn) with AI-optimized captions, hashtags, and post times.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Social media management tools $17.7B by 2030
- **Target**: 200M+ content creators globally
- **Problem**: Managing 5+ platforms manually is overwhelming

## Key Features
1. **One-Click Multi-Post**: Upload once, publish everywhere
2. **AI Caption Generator**: Platform-specific captions
3. **Hashtag Research**: Trending hashtags for each platform
4. **Best Time to Post**: AI predicts optimal posting times
5. **Content Calendar**: Visual planning for weeks/months
6. **Analytics**: Unified dashboard across all platforms

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: 1 profile, 10 posts/month
- **Creator**: $19/month - 3 profiles, unlimited posts
- **Professional**: $49/month - 10 profiles, team features, AI tools
- **Agency**: $149/month - 50 profiles, white-label, client management

**Path to $100K MRR**: 5,000 creators @ $20 average = $100K

## Competitive Advantages vs. Buffer/Hootsuite
- **AI-Powered**: Auto-generates content (Buffer doesn't)
- **TikTok Native**: Full TikTok support (competitors lacking)
- **Affordable**: $19 vs. $99 (Hootsuite)
- **Creator-Focused**: Built for individuals, not agencies

## Go-to-Market
