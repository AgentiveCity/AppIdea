# Blockchain Creator Monetization Platform

## Overview
A Web3 platform where creators can monetize through tokenomics, NFT subscriptions, and crypto payments without minimum followers. Own social network similar to "Own" app but more features.

## Key Features
1. **Creator Tokens**: Mint personal cryptocurrency for fans
2. **NFT Subscriptions**: Monthly NFT = membership access
3. **Token-Gated Content**: Exclusive content for token holders
4. **Crypto Payments**: Accept payments in ETH, USDC, etc.
5. **No Minimums**: Monetize from day 1 (vs. 10K followers on TikTok)
6. **Creator DAO**: Fans can vote on content direction

## Revenue Model
- **Transaction Fee**: 5% on all creator earnings (vs. 30-50% on Web2 platforms)
- **Token Launches**: $500 fee to launch creator token
- **Premium Features**: $49/month for analytics, custom branding

**Path to $100K MRR**: 2,000 creators earning average $5,000/month (5% cut = $10K fee each = $20M GMV/year)

## Viral Mechanics
- **Success Stories**: "I earned $10K in first month"
- **Airdrop Campaigns**: Free tokens to early supporters
- **Creator Showcases**: Top earning creators featured

## Competitive Advantages vs. Patreon/OnlyFans
- **Lower Fees**: 5% vs. 30-50%
- **Crypto Native**: Appeals to Gen Z/Web3 audience
- **Ownership**: Creators own their tokens/NFTs
- **Decentralized**: Not dependent on platform
