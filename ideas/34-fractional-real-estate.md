# Fractional Real Estate Investment App

## Overview
An app that allows users to invest in commercial real estate with as little as $100. Own fractions of apartment buildings, office spaces, and earn rental income.

## Key Features
1. **Low Minimums**: Invest starting at $100
2. **Curated Properties**: Vetted commercial properties
3. **Passive Income**: Monthly dividend payments from rent
4. **Secondary Market**: Sell shares before property sells
5. **Portfolio Diversification**: Invest in 10+ properties
6. **Transparency**: See property details, financials

## Revenue Model
- **Management Fee**: 1-2% annual AUM
- **Transaction Fee**: 1% on purchases
- **Performance Fee**: 10% of profits above benchmark

**Path to $100K MRR**: $120M AUM @ 1% annual fee = $100K monthly

- Broker-dealer license or partnership
- Expensive compliance ($1M+ setup)
