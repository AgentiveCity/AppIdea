# Creator Analytics & Growth Intelligence Tool

## Overview
An analytics platform for creators that tracks growth across all platforms, predicts viral content, and provides actionable insights to grow faster.

## Market Opportunity
- **Target**: 50M+ professional creators
- **Problem**: Platform analytics are siloed, no growth advice
- **Opportunity**: Creators pay for tools that grow their audience

## Key Features
1. **Unified Dashboard**: All platforms in one view
2. **Growth Predictions**: "Posting at 3 PM increases engagement 40%"
3. **Content Analysis**: What's working, what's not
4. **Competitor Tracking**: Monitor 5 competitors
5. **Viral Score**: Predict which posts will go viral
6. **Sponsorship Calculator**: "You should charge $X per post"
