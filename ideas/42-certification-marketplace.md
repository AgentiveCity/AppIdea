# Professional Certification Marketplace

## Overview
A marketplace for professional certifications and courses with AI-powered learning paths. Aggregates courses from multiple providers (Coursera, Udemy, etc.) and recommends optimal path.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Professional certification market $30B+
- **Trend**: Continuous learning is career necessity
- **Problem**: 1,000s of courses, hard to know which ones are worth it

## Key Features
1. **Certification Paths**: "Become AWS Certified in 3 months"
2. **Course Aggregation**: Compare courses from all platforms
3. **AI Recommendations**: Best courses for your goals
4. **Study Groups**: Connect with others pursuing same cert
5. **Practice Exams**: Test prep for certifications
6. **Job Matching**: Companies hiring for your certifications

## Revenue Model
- **Affiliate Commission**: 20-30% from course providers
- **Premium**: $19/month - Study groups, premium resources
- **Business**: $50/employee/month - Corporate training management

**Path to $100K MRR**: $500K course sales (20% = $100K) + 2,000 Premium users

## Viral Mechanics
- **Certification Success**: "I passed AWS exam!"
- **Study Group Growth**: Invite friends to study together
- **Job Wins**: "Got job thanks to certification"

## Timeline to $100K MRR: 14-18 months
