# Sleep Optimization & Smart Alarm App

## Overview
An AI-powered sleep app that analyzes sleep patterns, provides personalized recommendations, and wakes you at optimal time in sleep cycle. Uses phone sensors and optional wearable integration.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Sleep apps part of $11.27B wellness app market
- **Problem**: 1 in 3 adults don't get enough sleep, $411B annual productivity loss
- **Trend**: Sleep tracking is fastest growing wellness category

## Key Features
1. **Sleep Tracking**: Audio analysis (snoring, movement) without wearable
2. **Smart Alarm**: Wake during light sleep (feel more refreshed)
3. **Sleep Score**: Nightly rating with improvement suggestions
4. **Personalized Tips**: "Avoid caffeine after 2 PM"
5. **Sleep Sounds**: White noise, rain, meditation
6. **Trend Analysis**: Track sleep quality over months

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: Basic tracking, 3 sleep sounds
- **Premium**: $4.99/month or $39.99/year - Smart alarm, all sounds, trends

**Path to $100K MRR**: 20,000 Premium users @ $4.99 = $100K

## Viral Mechanics
- **Sleep Score Sharing**: "I got 95 sleep score!"
- **Streak Tracking**: "30 nights of good sleep"
- **Comparisons**: "You sleep better than 78% of users"

## Competitive Advantages
- **No Wearable Needed**: Phone-based (competitors require watch)
- **Cheaper**: $40/year vs. $70 (Sleep Cycle)
- **Better AI**: Personalized recommendations
