# Micro-Investing & Savings Automation

## Overview
An app that automatically invests spare change from purchases. Rounds up transactions to nearest dollar and invests the difference. Acorns competitor.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Micro-investing apps 20M+ users
- **Success**: Acorns has 9M+ users, $3B AUM
- **Psychology**: "Set it and forget it" drives adoption

- **AUM Fee**: 0.25% annual on invested assets

- **Better UX**: Simpler interface
- **Crypto Option**: Invest in crypto (Acorns lacks this)
