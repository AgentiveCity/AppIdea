# AI Meeting Summarizer & Action Tracker

## Overview
An AI assistant that joins meetings (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams), automatically transcribes, summarizes key points, and extracts action items. Integrates with project management tools to create tasks.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Meeting productivity tools $5B+ and growing
- **Problem**: Professionals spend 23 hours/week in meetings, 70% are unproductive
- **Trend**: Hybrid work = more virtual meetings
- **Cost**: Manual note-taking wastes 30 minutes per meeting

## Key Features
1. **Auto-Join Meetings**: Bot joins via calendar integration
2. **Real-Time Transcription**: Live transcript with speaker identification
3. **AI Summary**: 5-bullet summary of 60-minute meeting
4. **Action Items**: Automatically extract "TODO" items with owners
5. **Sentiment Analysis**: Detect agreement, concerns, confusion
6. **Integration**: Create Jira/Asana tickets from action items
