# Sustainable & Second-Hand Goods Marketplace

## Overview
A marketplace for buying/selling sustainable, eco-friendly, and second-hand goods. Combines Poshmark, ThredUp, and Etsy for all sustainable products.

- **Trend**: 73% of Gen Z prefer sustainable brands

## Key Features
1. **Unified Marketplace**: Clothes, furniture, electronics, all in one
2. **Sustainability Score**: Rate products on environmental impact
3. **Carbon Offset**: Option to offset shipping emissions
4. **Authentication**: Verify eco-certifications
5. **Circular Economy**: Trade-in program for old items
6. **Community**: Tips on sustainable living

## Revenue Model
- **Commission**: 10-15% on sales
- **Subscription**: $9.99/month for sellers (reduced commission to 5%)
- **Carbon Offsets**: $1-5 per order (optional add-on)

**Path to $100K MRR**: $1M GMV/month (10% = $100K)

- **Sustainability Impact**: "You've saved 50kg of CO2!"

## Competitive Advantages
- **All-in-One**: Not just clothes (Poshmark) or crafts (Etsy)
- **Sustainability Focus**: Purpose-built for eco-conscious
- **Carbon Tracking**: Quantify environmental impact
