# Decentralized Social Media Platform

## Overview
A Web3 social media platform where users own their data, earn crypto for engagement, and have governance rights. No censorship, no algorithm manipulation.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Social media users 5B+, growing distrust of centralized platforms
- **Trend**: Web3 social (Lens Protocol, Farcaster) gaining traction
- **Value Prop**: Users earn from their content (not platform)

## Key Features
1. **Data Ownership**: Users own content on blockchain
2. **Token Rewards**: Earn tokens for posts, likes, comments
3. **DAO Governance**: Token holders vote on platform rules
4. **No Ads**: Monetization through tokens, not ads
5. **Cross-Platform**: Content portable across Web3 apps
6. **Censorship-Resistant**: Decentralized storage (IPFS)

## Revenue Model
- **Transaction Fees**: 1-2% on token transactions
- **Premium Features**: $10/month for enhanced profiles
- **Token Launch**: Platform token ($SOCIAL) - founders hold 20%

**Path to $100K MRR**: Challenging, requires massive user base. Focus on token value appreciation instead.

## Challenges
- **Complexity**: Web3 is confusing for average users
- **Regulation**: Crypto regulations uncertain
- **Network Effects**: Hard to compete with established platforms
- **Moderation**: How to handle illegal content without centralization?

## Competitive Advantages
- **True Ownership**: Users actually own their content
- **Monetization**: Creators earn more (no platform cut)
- **Decentralization**: Can't be shut down

## Timeline to $100K MRR: 24-36+ months (network effects take time)
