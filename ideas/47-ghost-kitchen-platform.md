# Ghost Kitchen Multi-Brand Ordering Platform

## Overview
A delivery-only food ordering platform that aggregates multiple virtual restaurant brands from ghost kitchens into one unified app. Users can order from multiple "restaurants" in a single order, all prepared in the same kitchen for maximum efficiency.

## Market Opportunity

### Market Size
- Delivery-only restaurant industry: $177B+ by 2032
- Ghost kitchen market: $71B by 2027 (growing 12% annually)
- Online food delivery: $15.61B in Canada, $200B+ globally
- 75% of customers order last-minute meal delivery

### Problem Statement
- Ordering from multiple restaurants = multiple delivery fees
- Ghost kitchens operate 5-10 brands but customers don't know
- Existing apps (DoorDash, Uber Eats) charge 25-30% commission
- Long delivery times from distant restaurants
- No transparency about food origin

## Target Audience

### Consumers
- Urban professionals (25-45) ordering delivery 3+ times/week
- Families wanting variety (kids want pizza, parents want salad)
- Late-night eaters (ghost kitchens often open later)
- Budget-conscious diners (avoiding multiple delivery fees)

### Ghost Kitchen Operators
- Multi-brand ghost kitchen operators
- Restaurant owners expanding to delivery-only
- Cloud kitchen facility owners
- Virtual restaurant entrepreneurs

## Key Features

### Consumer App
1. **Multi-Brand Cart**: Order burgers + sushi + salad in one checkout
2. **Kitchen Transparency**: See which brands come from same kitchen
3. **Bundle Deals**: "Complete meal from 3 brands: $25"
4. **Real-Time Tracking**: Live order progress and driver location
5. **Dietary Filters**: Vegan, gluten-free, keto, halal, etc.
6. **Subscription**: "Unlimited delivery for $9.99/month"

### Kitchen Dashboard
7. **Multi-Brand Management**: Manage 10 brands from one interface
8. **Unified Orders**: All orders from different brands in one queue
9. **Menu Optimization**: AI suggests best-selling items
10. **Inventory Sync**: Track ingredients across all brands
11. **Analytics**: Revenue by brand, peak times, customer preferences

## Viral Mechanics
- **Variety Sharing**: "I ordered from 5 restaurants in one order!"
- **Bundle Challenges**: "Try all 8 brands for free delivery"
- **Referral Program**: Give $10, get $10 in credits

- **Loyalty Rewards**: Order from 5 different brands → free delivery

## Revenue Model

### Commission Structure (Lower than competitors)
- **Per Order**: 15% commission (vs. 25-30% on DoorDash/Uber Eats)
- Ghost kitchens save money, pass savings to customers
- Volume makes up for lower percentage

### Additional Revenue
- **Delivery Fee**: $2.99-5.99 per order
- **Subscription**: $9.99/month for unlimited free delivery
- **Advertising**: Ghost kitchens pay to be featured
- **Kitchen Services**: Software for kitchen management ($199/month)
- **Data Insights**: Sell anonymized data to food brands

### Path to $100K MRR
- **Scenario 1: Commission**
  - 50,000 orders/month @ $25 average = $1.25M GMV
  - 15% commission = $187K revenue
- **Scenario 2: Mix**
  - $75K commissions + $25K subscriptions + $20K kitchen software
- **Target**: 2,000 orders/day across 50 ghost kitchens

## Technical Stack
- **Consumer App**: React Native (iOS & Android)
- **Kitchen Dashboard**: React, Next.js
- **Backend**: Node.js, Python (Django/FastAPI)
- **Real-Time**: WebSockets for order tracking
- **Payments**: Stripe Connect (marketplace model)
- **Maps**: Google Maps API for routing
- **Database**: PostgreSQL, Redis for caching
- **Infrastructure**: AWS, CloudFront CDN

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Pilot City
- Launch in ONE city (e.g., Miami, Austin, Denver)
- Partner with 10 ghost kitchens (50+ virtual brands)
- Offer zero commission for first 3 months
- Focus on density (all kitchens in 5-mile radius)
- Target: 1,000 orders/week

### Phase 2 (Months 4-6): Market Domination
- Expand to 50 ghost kitchens in same city
- Launch marketing campaign
- Partner with CloudKitchens (largest operator)
- Prove unit economics before expanding
- Target: 5,000 orders/week

### Phase 3 (Months 7-12): Multi-City Expansion
- Expand to 3-5 additional cities
- Focus on density: Better to own 3 cities than be in 20
- Recruit ghost kitchen partnerships via B2B sales
- Launch subscription tier
- Target: 30,000 orders/week

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risks
- DoorDash/Uber Eats have massive marketing budgets
- Ghost kitchens might prefer established platforms
- Food quality varies across brands
- Regulatory challenges (food safety, licensing)
- Last-mile delivery logistics

### Mitigation
- Focus on ghost kitchen-specific features
- Lower commission attracts price-sensitive operators
- Quality ratings per brand + kitchen
- Partner with licensed facilities only
- Use existing delivery services (gig workers) or partner
