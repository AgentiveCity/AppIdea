# Corporate Wellness Platform for Remote Teams

## Overview
A comprehensive wellness platform for remote/hybrid companies covering mental health, fitness, nutrition, and team bonding. B2B SaaS for HR departments.

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Corporate wellness $90B+ globally
- **Trend**: 85% of companies offering wellness programs post-COVID
- **ROI**: $3.27 return for every $1 spent on wellness
- **Remote**: Traditional wellness (gym memberships) doesn't work for WFH

## Key Features
1. **Mental Health**: On-demand therapy, meditation, stress management
2. **Fitness Challenges**: Team competitions, step challenges
3. **Nutrition Coaching**: AI meal plans, healthy recipes
4. **Social Connection**: Virtual coffee chats, team events
5. **Wellness Points**: Gamification, redeem for rewards
6. **Analytics Dashboard**: HR tracks participation, ROI

## Revenue Model
- **Per Employee**: $10/employee/month
- **Minimum**: $500/month (50 employees)

**Path to $100K MRR**: 10,000 employees across companies @ $10 = $100K

## Competitive Advantages
- **Remote-First**: Built for distributed teams (not retrofitted gym membership)
- **Holistic**: Mental + physical + social (competitors focus on one)
- **Engaging**: Gamification drives participation

## Go-to-Market
1. **LinkedIn**: Target HR leaders
2. **Case Studies**: ROI-focused content
3. **Pilots**: Free 3-month trials for select companies
