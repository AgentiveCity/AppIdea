# Mental Health & AI Therapy Chatbot

## Overview
An AI-powered mental health companion providing 24/7 emotional support, CBT exercises, mood tracking, and crisis intervention. Accessible alternative to expensive therapy ($100-300/session).

## Market Opportunity
- **Market Size**: Mental health apps market growing to $17.5B by 2030
- **Problem**: 60% of people with mental health needs don't seek help (cost, stigma, access)
- **Demand**: Headspace (100M+ users), Calm (100M+), BetterHelp (4M+ subscribers)
- **Opportunity**: AI makes therapy accessible to everyone

## Key Features
1. **AI Therapy Chat**: CBT-based conversations, empathetic responses
2. **Mood Tracking**: Daily check-ins with insights over time
3. **Crisis Detection**: Escalate to human counselor if needed
4. **Guided Meditations**: Audio-guided sessions (5-20 minutes)
5. **Journaling**: Prompted reflections with AI insights
6. **Progress Reports**: Weekly mental health summaries

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: 5 AI chat sessions/month, basic mood tracking
- **Premium**: $14.99/month - Unlimited chat, all features
- **Premium Plus**: $29.99/month - + Monthly video session with human therapist

**Path to $100K MRR**: 7,000 Premium users @ $14.99 = $105K
